## 🎨 DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### **Typography Hierarchy**
- **H1**: 68-80px (Titles)
- **H2**: 48-56px (Section headers)
- **H3**: 32-36px (Sub-headers)
- **Body**: 22-28px (Content)
- **Small**: 20-24px (Notes)

### **Color Psychology**
**Education Template**:
- Green: Growth, learning, nature
- Orange: Energy, enthusiasm, creativity
- White: Clean, simple, focus

**Business Template**:
- Navy: Trust, professionalism, stability
- Blue: Innovation, technology, confidence
- Purple: Premium, creative, strategic
- Red: Urgency, passion, action
- Green: Success, growth, profit

### **Spacing & Layout**
- **Padding**: 30-50px in boxes
- **Margins**: 20-40px between elements
- **Grid gaps**: 25-30px
- **Border radius**: 10-15px (modern feel)

### **Visual Hierarchy**
1. **Primary content**: Large font, bold, colored
2. **Secondary content**: Medium font, regular
3. **Tertiary content**: Small font, muted color
4. **Supporting elements**: Icons, shapes, backgrounds

## 🚀 CÁCH SỬ DỤNG

### **Bước 1: Chọn Template**
1. Vào **Slide Bài Giảng**
2. Scroll xuống phần "Templates Mẫu"
3. Thấy 2 templates mới:
   - 📚 Bài Giảng Giáo Dục - 10 slides
   - 💼 Business Presentation - 10 slides

### **Bước 2: Tạo Presentation**
1. Click **"Sử dụng Template"**
2. Hệ thống tự động load 10 slides
3. Mỗi slide đã có:
   - Layout hoàn chỉnh
   - Nội dung mẫu
   - Màu sắc phù hợp
   - Animations

### **Bước 3: Customize**
1. **Edit text**: Click vào element → Sửa nội dung
2. **Replace images**: Upload ảnh mới
3. **Adjust colors**: Dùng theme hoặc color palette
4. **Add/remove elements**: Toolbar bên trái
5. **Reorder slides**: Drag & drop trong sidebar

### **Bước 4: Present**
1. Click **"Trình Chiếu"**
2. Fullscreen presentation
3. Navigation: Arrow keys, Space, ESC
4. Professional delivery!

### **File Structure**
```json
{
  "id": "template_education_complete",
  "name": "📚 Bài Giảng Giáo Dục - Hoàn Chỉnh",
  "description": "...",
  "category": "education",
  "theme": "education",
  "thumbnail": "📚",
  "slides": [
    {
      "id": "slide_1",
      "name": "Title Slide",
      "background": "linear-gradient(...)",
      "elements": [...]
    }
  ]
}
```

### **Element Types Used**
- ✅ `heading`: Titles, headers
- ✅ `text`: Rich HTML content
- ✅ `math`: KaTeX formulas
- ✅ `shape`: Decorative boxes
- ✅ Animation: 7 types

### **Animations Applied**
- `fadeIn`: Smooth appearance
- `slideInUp/Down/Left/Right`: Directional entry
- `zoomIn`: Attention-grabbing
- `bounceIn`: Playful (used sparingly)
